Aven Home Equity Card is a home equity line of credit featuring fixed rate plans and a variable rate revolving line of credit, each with its own annual percentage rate ("APR"). APR is the cost of credit as a yearly rate and does not include costs other than interest. The revolving line of credit's variable APR is based on the Wall Street Journal prime rate ("Index"). The Index is published periodically in the Money Rates section of the Wall Street Journal. As of Sep 26th, 2025 EST, the Prime Rate is 7.25%. Your variable APR is based on the Index plus a margin that takes into account your creditworthiness, collateral value, etc. Best rates are available only to the highest qualified borrowers on their primary residence. The fixed-rate, fixed-term plans' (also referred to as Aven Simple Loans) APR is based on your variable APR and other factors at the time you agree to the Aven Simple Loan. The fixed APR will not change during the plan term. For cash outs and balance transfers, there is a fee of 2.5% of the amount transferred. Aven's home equity lines of credit have no application, annual, maintenance, or origination fees. Depending on your property's location, there may be recording fees and other closing-related costs, including attorney's fees. See pricing and terms for details. All fees are subject to change.

Aven Home Equity Cash is a home equity line of credit featuring fixed rate plans alongside a variable rate revolving line of credit, each with its own annual percentage rate ("APR"). APR is the cost of credit as a yearly rate and does not include costs other than interest. The variable APR, which applies to the revolving line of credit, is based on the Wall Street Journal prime rate ("Index"). As of Sep 26th, 2025 EST, the rate is 7.25%. Your variable APR is based on the Index plus a margin that takes into account your creditworthiness, collateral value, etc. Best rates are available only to the highest qualified borrowers. The fixed APR, which applies to the fixed rate plans (also referred to as Aven Simple Loans), is based on your variable APR and other factors at the time you agree to the Aven Simple Loan. The fixed APR for an Aven Simple Loan will not change during the plan term. Aven Home Equity Cash requires an initial cash out transfer of the full credit line in accordance with the account agreement. For that initial cash out transfer, you are charged a fee of the amount drawn as provided in the account agreement. There are no fees for subsequent cash outs or balance transfers. Aven's home equity lines of credit have no application, annual, or origination fees. Depending on your property's location, there might be recording fees and other closing-related costs, including attorney's fees. See the account agreement for details. All fees are subject to change.
